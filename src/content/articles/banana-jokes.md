## Classic one-liners

- Why did the banana go to the doctor? Because it wasn't **peeling** well.
- What do you call two banana peels? A pair of **slippers**.
- Why don't bananas ever get lonely? They hang around in **bunches**.
- How do you make a banana split? Cut it in **two**.
- What's the easiest way to make a banana milkshake? Take a banana to a scary movie.

> *Banana who?*
