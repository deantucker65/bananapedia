The banana's fame has spawned an entire economy of merchandise. This is a living list — categories to explore and curate over time. (Add your own favorite finds and affiliate links here as the site grows.)

## Kitchen & practical

- **Banana slicers** — the single most famously reviewed product on the internet, thanks to thousands of gloriously sarcastic customer reviews
- **Banana holders / hooks** — countertop stands that slow ripening by letting fruit hang
- **Banana keepers** — single-banana travel cases to prevent lunchbox bruising

## Apparel & style

- Banana-print shirts, socks, and pajamas
- The inflatable **banana costume** — a party and marathon-running staple
- Enamel pins and patches

## Toys & novelty

- Plush bananas (including ones with faces)
- The **dancing/singing banana** desk toy
- Squishy stress-relief bananas

## Homage art

- Prints riffing on Warhol's *Velvet Underground* banana
- Cattelan-inspired "duct-taped banana" wall art (for the truly committed)
